About

Growing up between countries taught me something most people learn from textbooks — the same product needs a completely different story depending on who's buying it.

That instinct became my career.

At Aprilskin, I took over the US market with no team. Launched products, rebuilt the e-commerce site for American shoppers, built the email system from zero, and grew the brand for two years. I treated it like my own company.

The thread: I bridge the gap between a product and the people who should be buying it.

The Challenge

Olive Young expanding into SEA — needed performance marketing across Singapore, Philippines, and Malaysia. Each market had different consumer behaviors, price sensitivities, and content preferences.

What I Did
  • Managed ad accounts across Meta and TikTok in all three markets
